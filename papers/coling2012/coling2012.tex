\abstractEn{In this paper we propose a hybrid method for generating referring expressions that adapts a logic-based algorithm to consider probabilities learned from corpora. The algorithm is not only able to generate different referring expressions for the same target in a frequency similar to that observed in corpora but is also able to generate new natural sounding descriptions. The resulting algorithm is non-deterministic in nature and is able to generate the kind of overspecification observed in human produced referring expressions. We compare our results with human-produced descriptions and descriptions generated by a classical logic-based algorithm.}
